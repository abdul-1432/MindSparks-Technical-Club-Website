Our Dignities

Sri. Teegala Krishna Reddy

Chairman

Founded in 2001, TKR Educational Society, led by former Hyderabad Mayor Sri. Teegala Krishna Reddy, prioritizes academic excellence. Originating from Meerpet Village, aiming to fulfill the educational aspirations of youth in Andhra Pradesh and beyond.

Dr. D. V. Ravi Shankar

Principal

Dr. D. V. Ravi Shankar, TKR College of Engineering & Technology Principal, earned his AMIE in 1987 and M.Tech in Materials Engineering from NIT, Suratkal in 1994. He presented his M.Tech thesis at ISRO, Bangalore, and completed his Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering in 2010 at JNT University.

Dr. A. Suresh Rao

Vice Principal

Dr. A. Suresh Rao, a Computer Science & Engineering PhD from NIT Warangal (2015), serves as Vice Principal, Dean of Academics, and Head of CSE at TKRCET. His expertise enriches the department, fostering academic excellence and leadership.
