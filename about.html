Shaik Shoieb Sulthan

Shaik Shoieb Sulthan, the visionary founder of Mind Sparks Club, has carved a niche for himself in the educational realm with his innovative approach. As a passionate advocate for nurturing young talent, Sulthan established the club to empower freshly enrolled B.Tech students. His brainchild, approved by the prestigious AICTE, focuses on honing crucial skills through technical events, group discussions, and quizzes.
